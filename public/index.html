Pros and Cons

Standard Pro Con
X10 No new devices, sort of Lots of manual electrical work = dangerous
Wifi Ubiquitous Piece meal
Insteon Commercially available Expensive, proprietary

And the winner is...

Z-Wave

Side note: the real winner...

The real "winner" in the automation space will be a device that speaks all of these protocols.

Why Z-Wave

  • Mesh network
  • Wireless
  • Lightweight
  • "easy" to work with

Infrastructure

  • Hub / Controller - receives communication via HTTP, speaks Z-Wave
  • Devices - receive Z-Wave signals

Available Devices

  • Thermostats
  • Light switches
  • Wall outlets
  • Motorized blinds
  • Door locks
  • Irrigation control
  • Likely more...
