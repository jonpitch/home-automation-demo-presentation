The current state of home automation

Lots of options

Expensive!

  • Some smart components are 5-10x as expensive as their non-smart counterparts
  • On top of being expensive, they're ugly
  • Who wants to replace all of their light switches and outlets?

Standards

  • X10
  • Wifi
  • Insteon
  • Z-Wave
  • Zigbee
  • Rube Goldberg machines

Pros and Cons

Standard Pro Con
X10 No new devices, sort of Lots of manual electrical work = dangerous
Wifi Ubiquitous Piece meal
Insteon Commercially available Expensive, proprietary
Z-Wave / Zigbee Wireless, Seamless Expensive

And the winner is...

Z-Wave

Side note: the real winner...

The real "winner" in the automation space will be a device that speaks all of these protocols.

Why Z-Wave

  • Mesh network
  • Wireless
  • Lightweight
  • Easy to work with

Infrastructure

  • Hub / Controller - receives communication via HTTP, speaks Z-Wave
  • Devices - receive Z-Wave signals

Available Devices

  • Light switches
  • Wall outlets
  • Motorized blinds
  • Door locks
  • Irrigation control
  • Likely more...
